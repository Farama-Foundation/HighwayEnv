highway_env.envs.merge_env module¶

class highway_env.envs.merge_env.MergeEnv(config=None)¶

Bases: highway_env.envs.common.abstract.AbstractEnv

A highway merge negotiation environment.

The ego-vehicle is driving on a highway and approached a merge, with some vehicles incoming on the access ramp. It is rewarded for maintaining a high velocity and avoiding collisions, but also making room for merging vehicles.

_reward(action)¶
The vehicle is rewarded for driving with high velocity on lanes to the right and avoiding collisions, but an additional altruistic penalty is also suffered if any vehicle on the merging lane has a low velocity.
Parameters:action – the action performed
Returns:the reward of the state-action transition
_is_terminal()¶
